      When designing our Bark Life products we had two things in mind. Cute and practical. We want our customers to look good and be safe so we made all of our products out of dog friendly materials for those little monsters that can't help but chew on everything.

      Meet Yogi, the inspiration behind Bark Life Dog Boutique

      Yogi was 6 months old when we started Bark Life. He is a chewer and managed to ruin everthing we gave him or put on him. I wanted things that make him look good but that could also withstand those puppy teeth. And that's how Bark Life Dog Boutique came to be.

      Yogi and I want to thank you for supporting our business and we hope you and your four-legged friends are happy with your cute products.
